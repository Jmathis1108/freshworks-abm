Meet Freddy AI

AI that sees the whole stay,
not just one ticket

Most support AI treats every ticket in isolation. A guest complaint and a host complaint about the same stay are handled by two different agents who never compare notes. Freddy AI is built differently.

Freddy automatically merges host and guest context into a unified stay timeline. When a dispute arrives, your agent already has the full picture: what the host reported, when the guest checked in, what was communicated, and what a fair resolution looks like. No detective work. No blame-the-other-side calls.

People-first AI means both sides of a dispute feel heard. That is what builds trust in a marketplace. Not speed alone, but the sense that someone actually understood the situation before making a call.
